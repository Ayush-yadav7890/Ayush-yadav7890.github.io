I'm Ayush Yadav, a B.Tech ECE student at PSIT Kanpur (Batch 2027) with a deep passion for building technology that is both intelligent and visually compelling. My journey spans across frontend engineering, data analytics, and AI/RAG systems — giving me a rare full-stack perspective that bridges user experience with backend intelligence.

On the frontend, I engineer responsive, mobile-first interfaces using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+, ReactJS, and AngularJS — with a focus on reusable component architecture, accessibility, and clean code. I treat every pixel as intentional and every interaction as a chance to delight the user.

In the world of AI and data, I've built end-to-end RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines using LangChain, FAISS, and ChromaDB — processing 246K+ medical records for disease diagnosis. I'm fluent in Python (Pandas, NumPy), and comfortable writing complex SQL queries to extract meaningful insights from large datasets.

On the analytics side, I design Power BI dashboards powered by DAX measures that help businesses make data-driven decisions — from identifying 42% higher churn in telecom contracts to visualizing e-commerce performance across regions.

I stay ahead of the curve by actively using modern AI tools like Antigravity, Claude AI, Gamma AI, and Julius AI to accelerate development and prototype faster. With 7 industry certifications from Simplilearn, Udemy, Cisco, HackerRank and more — I'm committed to continuous learning and real-world application.
